Our Passion

As photographers, we tend to be visual learners. And it’s my job to make learning photography as easy as possible for you. So I thought to myself, “What better way to help photographers learn how to use their cameras, than by creating an infographic?” And that’s exactly what I did. I collaborated with an illustrator friend of mine, and together, we put together something that will make understanding exposure, and how cameras work, a whole lot easier!
